Privacy & Policy

Your privacy is paramount. This page outlines our commitment to protecting your personal data, detailing how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HEXA XOTICS platform.

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This includes personal information you voluntarily provide such as your name, email address, and contact details when you register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or fill out a contact form. We also gather technical data like IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems through automated means to enhance user experience and ensure security.

Additionally, usage data, including pages viewed and features accessed, is collected to understand how our services are used and to tailor future improvements. For payment processing, we collect transaction details, but sensitive financial information like credit card numbers is handled directly by secure third-party payment processors and is not stored on our servers.

The information we collect is primarily used to operate, maintain, and provide all features of the HEXA XOTICS service. This includes processing your requests, sending confirmations, and facilitating communication related to your account or services you have requested.

We also utilize data for internal analytics and research to understand usage patterns, diagnose technical problems, and improve the functionality and content of our platform. Periodically, we may use your contact information to send you updates, marketing communications, or promotional offers, provided you have opted in to receive such messages. You can always opt-out of these communications at any time.

Our website uses "cookies" and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and assist in our marketing efforts. Cookies are small data files placed on your device that allow us to recognize your browser and remember certain information, such as your preferences and login status.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until deleted) for various purposes, including authentication, security, and personalization. You have the option to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

Our service may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by HEXA XOTICS. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

We may also integrate with third-party tools for analytics, advertising, and functionality (e.g., Google Analytics, social media plugins). These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites and services. The data collected by these third parties is subject to their own privacy policies.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These rights may include the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You also have the right to object to the processing of your data, or to restrict its processing, under certain circumstances.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided on our Contact Us page. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Please note that we may require you to verify your identity before responding to such requests.
